## Surgical Drapes and Gowns industry Overview: Size and Growth Projections (Global & Regional Insights):

The surgical drapes and gowns market plays a critical role in maintaining infection control and patient safety during surgical procedures. These products are designed to create a sterile barrier between surgical sites and possible sources of contamination, including healthcare workers and the surrounding environment. The market is primarily driven by increasing surgical volume globally, heightened awareness of hospital-acquired infections (HAIs), and stringent regulatory guidelines regarding sterilization and hygiene in operating rooms. With a growing emphasis on patient safety and healthcare standards, hospitals and surgical centers are shifting toward high-performance, disposable surgical drapes and gowns made from advanced nonwoven materials.
