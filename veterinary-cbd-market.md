## Considerations and Challenges for AI in the Veterinary CBD industry

Key challenges include regulatory uncertainty, especially regarding safety claims, lack of large-scale clinical trials in animals, and difficulty in determining optimal dosing. Veterinarians often hesitate to recommend CBD due to limited legal clarity and insufficient scientific backing. Misinformation, variable product quality, and inconsistent labeling further complicate market trust. Data collection for AI-based tools also faces privacy and consent issues in veterinary settings.
